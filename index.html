FADHILI AGENCIES.

Fadhili is an (NGO) organisation that links employees with employers.

Fadhili employees and employers.

Fadhili is an NGO organisation that was formed with the aim of linking employees with employers easily.Fadhili eases employers the burden of advertisement for vacancies and interviews during replacement period.Fadhili will do all that on behalf of firms.Graduates also get a chance to stage their skills and get chances for good paying jobs.Youths with various skills register with Fadhili who then link them with employers.Firms can easily get the needed skills from Fadhili organisation.

We welcome youths with various skills to come and register with us for job chances.

At Fadhili Firms get the right skills needed.

Graduates have a platform to join Fadhili and get chances for various jobs offered.

Employers can easily get qualified employees from Fadhili Organization.
